Bookr

Bookr is an open educational resource for teachers. On Bookr, you can read reviews on, review, and download free textbooks on a wide range of subjects.

If you want free way to improve your teaching with peer reviewed textbooks, join Bookr today!

Our Mission

Bookr's team set out with a goal, making learning more accessible for people all around the world. No matter the location or financial situation, we wanted teachers to be able to freely teach with confidence knowing that the textbooks they were using were reliable. We are happy to say that we have empowered thousands teachers around the world and will continue to provide access to our open educational resource everyday.

How You Can Help

The best way to help Bookr is by telling people about Bookr! We want everyone to use our free resource. However, if you would like to make make sure Bookr stays up and running, a donation of any amount would be appreciated.
